# Contributors

Everyone listed here suggested an entry that was accepted into the AI-Pulse registry.

Entries are automatically picked up from GitHub Issues, scored by the [ai-pulse-agents](https://github.com/sam-89/ai-pulse-agents) pipeline, and promoted to the registry when they score ≥ 7/10. Accepted suggestions are credited here and in the `suggested_by` field of the registry JSON entry.

---

## How to get listed

Open a [**💡 Suggest a Registry Entry**](https://github.com/sam-89/ai-pulse/issues/new?template=suggest-entry.yml) issue. If your entry is accepted, your GitHub handle appears here automatically.
